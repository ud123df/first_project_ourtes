Logic Theory

Stoicism is a school of Hellenistic philosophy founded by Zeno of Citium in Athens in the early 3rd century BC. It is a philosophy of personal virtue and logic, and views the natural world as governed by reason.

Why we need philosophy

Philosophy is essential because it encourages critical thinking, self-reflection, and a deeper understanding of the world and our place in it. By exploring fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, ethics, and reason, philosophy helps individuals and societies clarify their beliefs and values. It provides tools for logical analysis and ethical decision-making, fostering open-mindedness and intellectual curiosity.
